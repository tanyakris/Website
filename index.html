Hi! I am a Sophomore at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I fell in love with Computer Science when I realized that I had the power and tools to create anything I wanted. I think it's absolutely amazing to think of an idea in my head and watch it come to life. I am currently interested in Artificial Intelligence and Web Development, but my interests are still broad. I am equally passionate about using Computer Science to make an impact on society, and I particularily want to focus on decreasing gender-bias and sexism in the world through education, because I strongly believe that knowledge power and power is liberating. Outside Computer Science, I love to learn about the universe and often find myself watching Neil Degrasse's or Michelle Thaller's lectures on astrophysics. I am currently working as a Research Assistant in UC Berkeley.

I like to watch
